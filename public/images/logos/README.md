## Recommended Specifications

- **Format:** PNG with transparent background preferred
- **Size:** 64x64px to 128x128px (will be displayed at 40x40px)
- **Style:** Official company logos or brand icons
- **Quality:** High resolution for crisp display

## Usage

The integrations page will automatically load these images. If a logo file is missing, a fallback placeholder will be shown.

## Sources

You can typically find official logos at:
- Company press kits or brand asset pages
- Official websites (often in /press or /brand sections)
- Logo databases like Brand Logo Database or similar resources

Always ensure you have proper licensing to use company logos.
